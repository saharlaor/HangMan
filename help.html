New Game Dark Mode Help

The game

  1. The game generates a random word and displays it as a series of lines representing the letters of the hidden word.
  2. You have 10 attempts to guess the word, one letter at a time.
  3. Each wrong letter you guess will appear red, and right guess is green.
  4. When you guess wrong another part is built.
  5. Guess the word before the man is hanged!

Game over

  1. If the whole word is revealed the man is freed and you win the game, a popup message will alert you of your win.
  2. If you guess 10 wrong letters the man gets hung and you lose
